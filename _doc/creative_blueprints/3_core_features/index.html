Custom Teams

Team name, short-code (e.g. "MUM"), custom brand colors — saved locally. Any team identity you want.

Quick-Add Roster

Paste 11 comma-separated names → instant playing XI. Full player profiles: role, batting & bowling style.

Ball-by-Ball Engine

Runs (+1–6) and all extras: Wide, No-Ball, Bye, Leg-Bye, Penalty. Wides & no-balls never consume legal balls.

🎯

Dismissal Engine

Bowled, Caught, LBW, Run Out, Stumped — each mapped to batsman, bowler, and fielder IDs from your roster.

↩

Infinite Undo / Redo

Event-sourcing engine — correct any ball at any point. No restarts, no data loss, full history preserved.

📈

Live Player Stats

Strike rate, economy, wickets, run rate — computed live from individual ball events per player, always accurate.
