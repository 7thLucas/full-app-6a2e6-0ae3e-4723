Why Cricket Scorer Pro Exists

!
The Problem

Local teams play serious cricket with no serious tool. Existing scorers:

  • ✕
    No custom rosters — pre-set team databases only; "Gully XI" doesn't exist
  • ✕
    Wides & no-balls wrongly consume legal-ball counts
  • ✕
    No undo — one typo forces a full match restart
  • ✕
    Stats are manually totalled — strike rate & economy are always wrong
✓
The Solution

Cricket Scorer Pro is built around your teams and your players — fully local, fully precise.

  • ✓
    Any team, any name, any color — custom rosters saved to device forever
  • ✓
    Correct extras — wides & no-balls never burn a legal ball
  • ✓
    Infinite undo/redo — fix any ball, any time, without restarting
  • ✓
    Every stat derived from ball events — strike rate & economy always accurate
