Foundation Validated

Manual cricket scoring is error-prone, lacks custom team support, and provides no real-time player statistics or undo capabilities.

The Problem

Manual cricket scoring suffers from human error, lacks custom team support, offers no detailed player statistics, and provides no recovery mechanism for mistakes. Teams cannot track individual player performance, and match data is fragmented across multiple sources.

The Solution

Scoring cricket matches manually is slow, inaccurate, and frustrating. Traditional apps lack support for custom teams and players, offer no ball-by-ball precision, and force users to restart if they make a mistake. Teams need a modern solution that handles complex dismissals, tracks every player's stats in real time, and scales to professional match requirements.

Target Audience

Cricket scorers, team captains, match organizers, and digital cricket enthusiasts managing custom teams and professional-grade matches

Personal Motivation

Transform cricket scoring from manual error-prone work into a modern, scalable digital system that captures every nuance of the game and empowers teams with actionable player statistics.
